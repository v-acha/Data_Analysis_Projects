About Baseball Viz

Baseball Viz is a platform designed to make baseball statistics easy to understand through interactive visualizations. Built as part of a class project, it offers detailed data on team and player stats, historical data, and advanced metrics. The site features user-friendly tools like customizable dashboards and educational resources to help users explore and appreciate the intricacies of baseball.

What You Can Find on Baseball Viz

1. Comprehensive Data Coverage

  • Team and Player Stats: Access detailed statistics for teams and players, covering various aspects of the game including batting, pitching, fielding, and more.
  • Historical Data: Explore historical data to see how the game and its players have evolved over time.
  • Advanced Metrics: Dive into advanced metrics that provide a deeper understanding of player performance and team dynamics.

2. Interactive Visualizations

  • Dynamic Charts and Graphs: Visualize complex data through interactive charts and graphs that make it easy to identify trends and patterns.
  • Heatmaps and Scatter Plots: Utilize heatmaps and scatter plots to analyze performance across different dimensions and variables.
  • Real-Time Updates: Get real-time updates on ongoing games and player statistics, ensuring you always have the latest information at your fingertips.

3. Educational Resources

  • Knowledge Base: Access a comprehensive knowledge base that explains various baseball statistics and metrics, helping you become a more informed fan.
  • Glossaries and Tutorials: Learn the terminology and methodologies used in baseball analytics with our detailed glossaries.

4. User-Friendly Interface

  • Customizable Dashboards: Create personalized dashboards to track your favorite teams and players.
  • Mobile-Friendly Design: Enjoy a seamless experience across all devices with our responsive and mobile-friendly design.
  • Easy Navigation: Navigate through the platform effortlessly with our intuitive and user-friendly interface.

Where We Get Our Data
