Batting Average vs. Expected Batting Average

This dashboard provides a detailed comparison of actual batting averages with expected batting averages for players. allows users to analyze performance discrepancies, identify players who are overperforming or underperforming relative to expectations, and explore team-wide trends across different seasons. By examining these metrics, fans and analysts can gain deeper insights into player performance and potential areas for improvement.
