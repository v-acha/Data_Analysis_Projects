Fastball Velocity Trends

This visualization examines the changes in average fastball velocity over time, starting from 2018 to the present.

The two dashboards allows users to select data from 2018 to 2024 for specific teams and pitch types (fc, ff, si). It provides a detailed analysis of average release speeds. Users can make single or multiple selections to compare teams' performances across different seasons and months. This helps to understand how teams compare in terms of fastball velocity, identify trends, and observe changes over time. Fans can use this to evaluate their team's pitching performance relative to competitors, while analysts can use this data for strategic decisions and performance improvements.
